Colouring this was a pain in the ass. Need to get better at using CSP to make it faster as I did it pretty suboptimally.

The ears are obscured by the hair more than I would like. But overall pretty satisfied with the drawing, it was pretty hard to draw. Drawing non-humans is hard :(.

Looking more closely at the reference drawing, I noticed that I ended up drawing her with a much broader build than I intended. Her shoulders should've been narrower and her head should've been a bit larger relative to the body. Probably like a 45% head to 55% body ratio would've looked better than what I have. What I have right now doesn't look too bad but could be improved of course :).
